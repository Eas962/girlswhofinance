Budget Planning:

The Tripod Approach:

When a person makes a budget they should divide their net income (after taxes)
into four groups:

  • 10% to debt reduction
  • 10% to building up a cash heard equal to six months of living expenses
  • 10% to retirement savings
  • 70% to living expenses
